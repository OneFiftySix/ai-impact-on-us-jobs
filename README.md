# AI Impact on the U.S. Job Market (2018–2025)

This project analyzes trends in AI adoption and its relationship to job creation and job displacement in the United States from 2018 to 2025.

## Repository Structure

ai-job-market-analysis/
│── notebooks/
│ └── 1st Mini Project.ipynb
│── presentation/
│ └── Copy of Masin Namwar 1st Mini Project.pdf
│── data/
│── images/
│── README.md
│── requirements.txt


## Key Questions
- How do AI-created jobs compare to job elimination?
- Which industries are at higher risk of automation?
- How has AI adoption grown over time?

## Tools & Technologies
- Python
- Jupyter Notebook
- Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib
